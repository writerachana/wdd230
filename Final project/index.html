Newport Beach, California

Newport Beach is a coastal city situated in Orange County, Southern California. It is known for its harbor, sandy beaches, and upscale waterfront communities. Newport Beach combines coastal elegance with a vibrant seaside lifestyle, making it a sought-after destination for those seeking luxury, outdoor activities, and a relaxed atmosphere by the sea. Newport Beach boasts several pristine beaches, including Newport Municipal Beach and Corona del Mar State Beach. These beaches are popular for sunbathing, surfing, and beach volleyball.
